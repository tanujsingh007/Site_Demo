About Us

zipCar is India’s largest mobility platform and one of the world’s largest ride-hailing companies, serving 250+ cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. The zipCar app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles across bikes. zipCar mobility offering in India is supplemented by its electric-vehicle arm, zipCar Electric; India’s largest fleet management business, zipCar Fleet Technologies and zipCar Skilling, that aims to enable millions of opportunities for youth. ZapCar was founded in Dec 2010 by Ankit Bhati with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
