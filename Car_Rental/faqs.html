FAQs

1. How much daily rental will I need to pay?
You will need to pay a daily rental amount between Rs.500 to Rs.1000 depending on your city and car model you have selected.
2. How do I pay my daily rental?
CarRental will automatically deduct your daily rental amount from your total earnings and transfer the rest through NEFT or Cash.
3. Will I be able to send manual requests for updating schedules?
No, manual or email request will not be possible.
4. Who will be the registered owner of the car?
The car will be registered under company's name - CarRental.
5. How can I attach my car with CarRental?
You can visit our office in your city. Our city team would be happy to guide you with the registration process.
6. How will I be paid?
All your outstanding payments at the end of the day are transferred to your bank account within the next 2 working days.
7. Who should I contact in case of any queries about my earnings?
You can call us or walk into our office for query resolution.
8. How much time does it take to complete the registration process?
Typically, it should take close to 2 hours for you to be ready to take your first booking.
9. How much will I be charged for registration?
Registration is free.
10. How old do I need to be to rent a car?
You must be atleast 20 years of age.
11. Can I book a oneway car rental?
Yes, oneway car rentals are quite common, as they give people even more freedom to explore and enjoy their rental.
12. How do I find the cheapest car rental deals?
We specialize in finding the cheapest car rental deals from major brands.
13. What kind of cars can I rent on CarRental?
On CarRental you can find deals on all types of rental cars including Premium, Low Emission, Economy, Luxury, Mini.
14. What documents do I need to get at the time of rental?
To rent a car, a valid driving license and current identity card are required.
15. Is it necessary that the car must be returned with full tank of fuel?
The car is rented with the full tank and should also be in such a state.
16. Can I purchase additional equipment?
Yes, we offer GPS, childseat, additional insurance, roof rack.
17. Are the cars Check-Auto Insured?
Yes, our cars have full liability insurance/ accident/ AC/ ASS.
18. What do I do when a car crash?
For events involving a conflict of road or car accident, please contact us.
19. Do I need to pick up the car from CarRental office?
All the cars are delivered and collected free of cost to the address specified at the time of booking.
20. Can I drop of the car at different location?
No, the car will be collected by our driver from the place where it was delivered.
